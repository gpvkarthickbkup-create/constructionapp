## SQFT-Based Estimation

The software uses a unique SQFT-based estimation model:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Total Sqft | Total construction area |
| Customer Rate/Sqft | What you charge the customer |
| Builder Rate/Sqft | What it actually costs you |
| Customer Estimate | Sqft × Customer Rate |
| Builder Estimate | Sqft × Builder Rate |
| Sale Amount | Final agreed price (entered after confirmation) |
| Estimated Profit | Customer Estimate - Builder Estimate |
| Actual Profit | Sale Amount - Actual Expenses |
| Profit Margin % | Automatically calculated |

## How It Works

### For Builders:
1. **Create a Site** — Enter project name, client details, SQFT, budget
2. **Add Expenses** — Record every purchase (cement, steel, labor, transport)
3. **Track Budget** — See real-time budget vs actual with progress bars
4. **Manage Payments** — Track what you've paid vendors and what's pending
5. **Collect from Clients** — Record customer payments with receipts
6. **Generate Reports** — Print professional reports for clients or internal use

### For Land Promoters:
1. **Register Land** — Enter area, purchase cost, expected sale value
2. **Divide into Plots** — Create plots with numbers, area, facing
3. **Track Development** — Record road, drainage, electricity costs
4. **Sell Plots** — Book plots for customers, track installments
5. **Calculate Profit** — See investment vs revenue vs profit in real-time
