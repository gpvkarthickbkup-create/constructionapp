## 🎯 What does this app do?

Datalytics Easy Build helps you **track every rupee spent on your construction sites**. Whether you're building a house, villa, apartment, or commercial building — this app tells you:

- 💰 How much money has been spent so far?
- 📦 How much went to cement, steel, sand, tiles?
- 👷 How much was paid for labor?
- 🏗️ Which site is costing the most?
- 📊 Are we within budget or over budget?
- ❌ How much is still pending to pay?
- 💵 How much has the customer paid?
- 📈 What is our profit?

---

## 📱 How to Use — Step by Step

### Step 1: Add Your Client
Go to **Clients** → Tap **+ Add Client**
Enter name and mobile number. That's it!

### Step 2: Create a Site
Go to **Sites** → Tap **+ Add Site**
- Select the client
- Enter site name (e.g., "Raju Villa - Velachery")
- Enter total sqft (e.g., 1500)
- Enter your rate per sqft to customer (e.g., ₹2,500)
- Enter your actual cost per sqft (e.g., ₹1,800)
- The app automatically calculates your **estimated profit!**

### Step 3: Add Vendors
Go to **More → Vendors** → Tap **+ Add**
Add all your suppliers:
- Cement dealer
- Steel supplier
- Sand supplier
- Labor contractor
- Transport company
- Electrician, plumber, etc.

### Step 4: Record Expenses (Daily)
Every time you buy material or pay someone:
- Tap the **+** button
- Select the site
- Select type (Material / Labor / Transport / Rental)
- Select item (Cement, Steel, Sand, etc.)
- Enter quantity and rate
- Select the vendor
- Mark as Paid / Unpaid
- Save!

**That's it!** The app tracks everything automatically.

### Step 5: Check Your Dashboard
Open the app → Dashboard shows:
- Total spend across all sites
- Material vs Labor breakdown
- Budget progress for each site
- Pending payments
- Today's and this month's spend

### Step 6: Collect Payments from Clients
Go to **Clients** → Tap client name
- See total collected so far
- Tap **Record Payment**
- Enter amount, payment type (Cash/UPI/Bank)
- Get a receipt!

### Step 7: View Reports
Go to **Reports** → Select any report:
- Site-wise expense
- Vendor-wise payment
- Pending payments
- Budget vs Actual
- Monthly spending

Print any report with your company logo!

The app calculates:
- Customer Estimate: **₹37,50,000**

As you add expenses, the app shows:
